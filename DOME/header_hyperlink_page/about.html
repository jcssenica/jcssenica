The DOME consists a reconstructed nature that hints to a speculative
environment in the near future that derived from the vision of the
producer and world building project. Taking place in a dome, audience
will find a reimagined tropical forests with gloomy, dimmed atmosphere
and humid climate. On the contrary, transparent, and chrome materials
are used to create surreal objects to emphasize a dreamy illusion. This
project hopes to illustrate an environment that invites the audience to
roam within the dark and bright and get inspired by the unique
perspective and imagination.
